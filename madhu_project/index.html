Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I report a lost item?
Go to the "Report Lost Item" section from the sidebar, fill in the details about your item including description, location, and date. Submit the form and we'll start looking for matches immediately.
How does the matching system work?
Our AI-powered system automatically compares lost and found items based on category, description, keywords, and location. When a potential match is found, both parties are notified immediately.
What should I do when I find an item?
Report it immediately using the "Report Found Item" form. Include clear photos and detailed description. Keep the item safe until the owner is identified and contact is established.
How long does it take to find a match?
Average match time is 2.4 days. Some items match within hours, while others may take longer depending on how detailed the description is and how many similar items are reported.
Is my personal information safe?
Yes! We use 256-bit encryption and never share your personal information. Contact details are only shared when a match is confirmed and both parties agree to connect.
